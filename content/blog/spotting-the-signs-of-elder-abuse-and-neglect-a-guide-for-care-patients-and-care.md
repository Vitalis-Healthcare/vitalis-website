Elder abuse and neglect are serious, yet often hidden, issues affecting millions of older adults across the U.S. At **Vitalis HealthCare**, we believe in creating a safe, respectful, and compassionate environment for every senior under our care. Whether you're a caregiver, family member, or care recipient, recognizing the signs of abuse or neglect is the first step in protecting vulnerable seniors.

### **⚠️ What is Elder Abuse?**

Elder abuse includes any intentional or negligent act that causes harm or distress to an older adult. It can occur at home, in facilities, or within the community, and it can take many forms:

* **Physical abuse** (hitting, restraining)
* **Emotional abuse** (yelling, threats, isolation)
* **Sexual abuse**
* **Financial exploitation**
* **Neglect** (failing to provide basic needs like food, hygiene, or medical care)

### **Common Warning Signs**

#### **For Care Patients and Family Members:**

* Unexplained bruises, cuts, or burns
* Sudden changes in mood or behavior (fear, withdrawal, anxiety)
* Poor hygiene, malnutrition, or dehydration
* Unpaid bills or unusual financial transactions
* Fear of a specific person or caregiver

#### **For Caregivers:**

* Care recipient appears fearful or unusually quiet around certain individuals
* Living conditions are unsanitary or unsafe
* Medications are over- or underused
* Family or facility staff is uncooperative or overly controlling

### **️ What You Can Do**

* **Speak up.** If you suspect abuse, report it immediately to Adult Protective Services or local authorities.
* **Check in regularly.** Frequent visits and communication with seniors can deter abuse and reveal warning signs.
* **Choose trusted care providers.** Work with licensed, reputable agencies like **Vitalis HealthCare**, where safety, dignity, and compassion are our top priorities.
