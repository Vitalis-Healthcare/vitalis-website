Minor wounds like cuts or scrapes can be treated from the home. However, wounds that cut deeper than a quarter inch or bleed continuously and excessively should be treated by a medical doctor.
The body is designed to heal and repair minor wounds by itself but with a little assistance, it is bound to heal faster and prevent scarring.
Here are a few ways to manage and care for your wound at home to prevent it from infection and enable proper healing;

1. **Get The Right Health Supplies**

2. **Keep Your Hands Clean**

3. **Cleanse The Wound With Mild Soap and Water**

4. **Disinfect The Wound With Antiseptic**

5. **Apply Antibiotic Ointment(s)**

6. **Protect The Wound**

7. **Keep The Wound Moist**

8. **Manage The Pain**

9. **Avoid Harsh Substances**

10. **Monitor For Signs Of Infection**
