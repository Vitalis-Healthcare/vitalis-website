When it comes to home care of their parents, many individuals feel finding the agency is the biggest challenge. It can be equally challenging to convince the parents to receive these services. There’s a lot of resistance in the beginning. Don’t worry; we’ve got your back.
If you live in the Silver Spring, Maryland area, here is a list of some effective ways to convince your parents to get personal care assistance services.

## **Start the discussion early**

Don’t inform them about the home care when you have finalized an agency. They should be aware of the process from the very beginning. You get enough time to repeat this topic, so that it doesn’t look like a sudden surprise.

## **Involve them in the process**

When you are interviewing the caregivers or searching the agencies, involve your parents in the process. This lets them express their needs and expectations. They can also tell you if they were comfortable talking to a caregiver or not. If not, try to identify the reasons.

## **Talk about the benefits**

Tell them they can visit their friends or public places easily if there’s personal care aide with them. Also talk about how personal care assistance services in Maryland will send people who will listen to their stories and play interesting games with them.

## **Start with household chores**

When you decide on a caregiver, invite them for a few days just to prepare meals and perform household chores. Encourage your loved ones to interact with them just for a few minutes daily. After a few days, a sense of familiarity will develop -- which increases the chances of acceptance.

## **Hire some reliable**

Many agencies hire only those professionals who are genuinely passionate about senior care. Such caregivers know how to put the seniors at ease when working with them. They are also great listeners and communicators.
