We are pleased to announce that Vitalis HealthCare, your favorite homecare agency, has just recently won the Best of Home Care- Employer of Choice Awards for outstanding caregiver satisfaction ratings.
Just in case you are wondering what this award is all about, let’s bring you up to speed.
The Best of Home Care- Employer of Choice award is based on caregiver ratings collected via telephone interviews by Home Care Pulse, an independent satisfaction research firm. Agencies that have earned this award are best-in-class for caregiver satisfaction.
The requirements to qualify for this award include;

* Being a Home Care Pulse Certified- Trusted Provider for a minimum of 6 consecutive months.
* Having an OSAT Score of 8.75+
* Having less than 15% of clients on “Do Not Call” for the year.

To much of our delight, according to these requirements and the ratings received from our customers, Vitalis Health Care managed to emerge as the winner of this award.
We are grateful to our customers for their trust and confidence in our services and we assure you that as a Caregiver Agency, our commitment and goal will continually remain to satisfy our customers with the best quality of compassionate and excellent home care services.
