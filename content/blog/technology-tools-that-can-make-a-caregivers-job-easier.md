Caregiving is a hands-on, heart-driven profession—but that doesn’t mean it has to be overwhelming. At **Vitalis HealthCare**, we believe in using smart, simple technology to support our caregivers and improve the quality of care for our clients across Maryland. One of the most effective tools we use is **AxisCare**, a caregiver-friendly software that makes scheduling, documentation, and communication smoother and more efficient.

### **1. Easy Clock-In/Clock-Out with GPS**

AxisCare allows caregivers to **clock in and out directly from their smartphones**, using secure GPS verification. This helps ensure accurate time tracking, while giving families peace of mind knowing their loved one is receiving timely care.

### **2. Real-Time Care Notes and Updates**

Caregivers can easily document services provided, note any changes in a client’s condition, and communicate important updates—all in real time. This helps streamline reporting and ensures better coordination with supervisors and family members.

### **3. Access to Client Information On-the-Go**

Need to check a client’s care plan, allergies, or medication list? AxisCare keeps vital information at your fingertips, so you can deliver personalized care safely and confidently—even when plans change.

### **️ 4. Clear Schedules and Reminders**

AxisCare provides caregivers with an up-to-date schedule, including visit times, client addresses, and task lists. This helps minimize confusion, reduce missed appointments, and support work-life balance.

### **Empowering Caregivers, Enhancing Care**
